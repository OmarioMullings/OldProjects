Welcome to Seneca College.
Thank you for your interest!

1- At Seneca, you'll benefit from rewarding opportunities in your search for a fulfilling career. Our goal is to work in partnership with you and industry to ensure your success. To accomplish this, we meet industry's constantly changing demands for a productive workforce. That means offering the leading-edge programs today that will support your success in the knowledge-based global economies of tomorrow.

2- What you'll experience at Seneca is a reflection of what life and work is about today - both here and around the world. We celebrate diversity - 65 languages are spoken daily in our hallways. We are results-driven - our most recent statistics show that 88% or our graduates find related work within six months of graduation. We offer programs in the highest growth occupations, from computer programming and software development to health care, science and financial services management.

3- Rely on our knowledgeable faculty and staff for an encouraging and supportive learning environment. They can help you make the right program choice to start your career.

4- At Seneca you'll learn essential skills for today's workplace...how to work in a team, to be responsive and to adapt to a changing work environment,whatever your career or program choice.

5- To ensure the best educational, career and networking opportunities, we are continually developing new partnerships with leading national and international companies.

6- You may choose to pursue further educational opportunities through our specialized post-diploma programs and a growing number of transfer agreements with universities. These options range from studies in Australia and British Columbia to the United States - as well as around the corner at York University.

7- To offer the best quality educational experience for today's world, we maintain the most up-to-date equipment, technology and teaching skills - and we've upgraded our facilities. Two new student residences at Newnham and King Campuses expand opportunities for student life on campus. In response to the growing demand for job-ready graduates with high-tech skills, we've launched our new, fully-wired Seneca@York campus on the York University site. This visionary campus, designed by renowned Canadian architect Raymond Moriyama, is home to our Schools of Computer Studies, Communication Arts, Biological Sciences and Applied Chemistry and our Centre of Professional Communications.

8- We look forward to welcoming you to Seneca College and wish you much success in all of your personal, educational and career goals.

9- We welcome the opportunity to help you start your career here.
